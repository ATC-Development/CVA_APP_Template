CVA Application · UI Prototypes

Design references for the ground-up rebuild of the Core Value Assessment application (React PWA + ASP.NET Core API). These are single-file HTML mockups with sample data — they define layout, interaction, and visual language for the build. They are not connected to any data source and nothing entered here is saved.

01

Dashboard

CVA_Dashboard.html

The landing experience. A three-role "hat" model — Mine (employee), My Team (supervisor), and HR Operations — selectable within one interface. Establishes the Stage Track as the system's signature visual and the modal patterns for record view, history, notes, pipeline detail, assign & start, and the org hierarchy browser.

  • Role switching
  • Stage Track (hero / row / aggregate)
  • Identity rail
  • Org hierarchy modal
  • Assign & start

02

CVA Form Screens

CVA_Form_Screens.html

The assessment lifecycle, one screen per stage. Six modes on a top switcher: Independent Review (dockable widget workspace — form, last CVA, PPM reference, notes with AI assist), Final Combine (three-column blend with divergence flags), HR Review (category-anchored notes), Returned to Primary (inline HR notes, mark-addressed), Employee Sign-off, and Executive Review.

  • Docked widgets + tray
  • Independent grading
  • Copy → Final
  • AI suggest-only
  • HR note flow
  • Signature dialogs
Open the form screens prototype
Ground rules reflected in these prototypes: reviewers grade independently and cannot see each other's drafts until both are signed · AI assistance is suggest-only (category mapping and phrasing) and never assigns or implies a rating · HR notes live in their own table and are rendered alongside the form, never merged into comment text · every lifecycle transition is an explicit, signed human action.
